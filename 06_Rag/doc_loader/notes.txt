Generative AI (Gen AI) - Comprehensive Guide

## Introduction
Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can generate text, images, code, audio, and more.
It has revolutionized industries including healthcare, education, software development, and entertainment.

## Key Concepts
- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- Neural Networks
- Transformers
- Large Language Models (LLMs)

## Popular Models
- GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer)
- BERT
- DALL·E
- Stable Diffusion

## Applications
1. Content Creation
2. Chatbots
3. Code Generation
4. Image Generation
5. Video Editing

## Working of Generative AI
Generative AI models are trained on large datasets and learn patterns.
They use probabilistic methods to predict the next token or pixel.

## Advantages
- Automation
- Creativity boost
- Time-saving

## Challenges
- Bias
- Hallucinations
- Ethical concerns

## Future Scope
Generative AI will play a major role in AGI development, personalized assistants, and autonomous systems.
